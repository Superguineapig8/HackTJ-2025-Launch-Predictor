Our Mission

Welcome!

We are Rocketry Hackers

This is a website made for any rocketry enthusuiast or team, we created this as a versatile site for anyone. If for example, you wish to launch a rocket on a specific day in a specific location, our algorithm shows you if that is a good time to launch, or whether or not you should reconsider. You need to insert some data values and our site does the rest. Our profesionally createdd ML algorithm will tell you the best launch window and will help speed up all of your persuits.
